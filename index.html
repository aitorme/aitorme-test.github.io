Aitor Egurtzegi

I am a PhD student at the Department of Comparative Language Science at the University of Zurich under the supervision of Prof. Balthasar Bickel,Prof. Martin Meyer and Dr. Sebastian Sauppe. I am writing my thesis on the ergativity of language comprehension and production, using experimental methods from psychology to study language typology, with a focus on Basque.

I hold a BA in Linguistics from the University of Barcelona where I graduated in 2014, an MSc in Human Language Science and Technology from the University of Malta, and an MSc in Language Science and Technology from Saarland University, both obtained in 2016 through the Language & Communication Technologies Erasmus Mundus program.
