Aitor Egurtzegi

I'm a PhD student at the Department of Comparative Language Science at the University of Zurich under the supervision of Prof. Balthasar Bickel, Prof. Martin Meyer and Dr. Sebastian Sauppe. I am writing my thesis on the ergativity of language comprehension and production, using experimental methods from psychology to study language typology, with a focus on Basque.
